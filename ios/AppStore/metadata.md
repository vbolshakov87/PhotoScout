## Description (4000 characters max)

PhotoScout is your AI-powered travel planning companion designed specifically for photographers. Whether you're chasing the perfect golden hour shot in the Dolomites or looking for hidden gems in Tokyo, PhotoScout helps you plan trips that maximize your photography opportunities.

**KEY FEATURES**

• AI Travel Assistant - Chat with our intelligent assistant to plan your perfect photography trip. Tell us your interests, duration, and style preferences, and get personalized recommendations.

• 94 Stunning Destinations - Explore curated destinations including 40 iconic cities and 54 breathtaking nature locations, from the Swiss Alps to Patagonia.

• Beautiful Trip Plans - Receive detailed HTML travel plans with day-by-day itineraries, best photography spots, optimal shooting times, and local tips.

• Cinematic Destination Images - Each destination features stunning AI-generated imagery in epic cinematic photography style to inspire your next adventure.

• Chat History - All your conversations are saved so you can revisit and refine your travel plans anytime.

• Trip Gallery - Browse your planned trips with beautiful destination cards and easy access to your itineraries.

**PERFECT FOR**

• Landscape photographers seeking dramatic natural locations
• Street photographers exploring vibrant cities
• Travel photographers planning multi-destination trips
• Photography enthusiasts looking for their next adventure
• Anyone who wants to combine travel with stunning photo opportunities

**DESTINATIONS INCLUDE**

Cities: Tokyo, Paris, New York, London, Rome, Barcelona, Amsterdam, Prague, Dubai, Singapore, Sydney, and 30 more iconic locations.

Nature: Dolomites, Swiss Alps, Norwegian Fjords, Iceland, Santorini, Banff, Yosemite, Grand Canyon, Patagonia, Bali, New Zealand, and 40+ more breathtaking landscapes.

Start planning your dream photography trip today with PhotoScout!
